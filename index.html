Our commitment to the economic energy transition - together with you!

TEXEP Ltd is committed to protecting the climate and the environment. TEXEP invents, develops and implements innovative solutions for the production and storage of renewable energy as well as the desalination and purification of water.

TEXEP builds on and extends a 45-year company history. The entrepreneurial spirit of two generations, father Mario and son André Posnansky, led to the realization of pioneering projects through the companies they founded and managed: SOLAIRE FRANCE SARL, SOLAIRE SUISSE Ltd and SUNSTYLE Ltd.

Soliaire France SARL

SOLAIRE FRANCE was founded in 2007 by André and Mario Posnansky with the purpose of development, financing, and turnkey realization of the Saint Charles Solaire power plant in Perpignan, France. The Saint Charles Solaire plant, realized in 2008–2011, with a capacity of 9 megawatts, an area of 70,000 m² and 97,000 installed solar shingles, is to date the largest building-integrated solar power plant in the world. Today, TEXEP is the parent company of SOLAIRE FRANCE with a stake in the Saint Charles Solaire power plant.

Sunstyle Ltd.

SUNSTYLE Ltd was majority-owned and managed by André Posnansky for 15 years, developed the patented electricity-producing solar roof of the same name, SUNSTYLE®, and realized a number of outstanding projects on the basis of the technology.
SUNSTYLE Ltd was sold in 2021. Convinced by the product, TEXEP continues to use the SUNSTYLE® solar roof system for building-integrated photovoltaics.
